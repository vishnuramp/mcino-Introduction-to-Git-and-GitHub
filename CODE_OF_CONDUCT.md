# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience.

## Our Standards
Respectful communication and constructive collaboration.

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive behavior may be reported.

## Enforcement Guidelines
Maintainers will review and investigate complaints.
